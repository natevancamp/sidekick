Buy smart out here

What to know before you buy an acreage home

Large-lot properties come with a few things city buyers never think about. Knowing them up front protects you, and it is exactly where a local broker earns their keep.

✦

Community water system

Todd Creek Farms is served by a community well system, so you get the acreage lifestyle without drilling or maintaining your own private well. I will explain how the shared water system works and what to expect for any home you consider.

✦

Septic systems

Many acreage homes use septic instead of sewer. I make sure the right inspections and permits are built into your offer.

✦

Utilities and internet

Propane, electric, and internet options can vary property to property. Know what you are getting before closing, not after.

✦

HOA and covenants

Todd Creek Farms has a homeowners association. I will walk you through the current dues and rules for any home you are considering.

✦

Outbuildings and zoning

Barns, shops, and horse facilities add real value and come with rules worth understanding before you buy or build.

✦

True neighborhood comps

Acreage homes are hard to value with online estimates. I price your offer against real local sales so you never overpay.

Todd Creek Farms buyer questions

Answers before you ask

How much do homes in Todd Creek Farms cost?

Recent sales have generally landed around $900k to $1.3M in mid-2026, though price varies significantly with acreage, home size, age, and finish level. Get on my listing alerts to see current homes and real prices as they come available.

How much do I need for a down payment?

Less than most people expect. Many buyers assume a home over $1 million requires 20 percent down, but that is not always true. There are jumbo loan options that allow as little as 5 percent down for qualified buyers, which can dramatically lower the cash you need upfront. I am happy to connect you with a trusted local lender who can show you exactly what you qualify for, with no obligation.

Is Todd Creek Farms in Brighton or Thornton?

Todd Creek Farms addresses are in Brighton, 80602, but the neighborhood is located in unincorporated Adams County rather than inside Brighton city limits. It is also often confused with Heritage Todd Creek, a separate 55+ gated golf community with Thornton addresses. They are not the same neighborhood, which matters for pricing and lifestyle.

Does Todd Creek Farms have an HOA?

Yes, the neighborhood has a homeowners association with dues and covenants. I will get you the current specifics for any home you are considering so there are no surprises.

Can you have horses in Todd Creek Farms?

The neighborhood is known for large, horse-friendly acreage lots, which is a big part of its appeal. Specific rules can vary by property, so always confirm before you buy. I will help you check.

What schools serve the area?

The area is served by Brighton-based School District 27J. Boundaries can change, so confirm the assigned schools for any specific address before you buy.

How far is it from Denver and the airport?

Roughly 20 minutes to Denver International Airport and about 30 minutes to downtown Denver via E-470, with the tollway just minutes from the neighborhood.
