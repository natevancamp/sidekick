What's my home really worth?

The five things that move a Todd Creek Farms price

An online estimate (Zillow "Zestimate," etc.) treats your acreage like a city lot. It's often off by six figures out here. A real valuation weighs:

  • Usable land & acreage: how much, how flat, fenced, irrigated?
  • Outbuildings & systems: barns, shops, and septic systems.
  • Views & privacy: front-range views and setback carry a premium.
  • Finish level: custom vs. builder-grade drives buyer perception.
  • True neighborhood comps: Todd Creek Farms sales only, not lumped-in data.
